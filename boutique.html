Our Story

Born from a
love of
craft.

Nithu's was born out of a deep admiration for India's textile heritage. Every saree we carry is sourced directly from weavers who pour generations of knowledge into their looms — from the silken drape of Kanjivaram to the breezy grace of Chanderi.

Our blouse boutique is a celebration of the modern woman — tailored, expressive, and rooted in culture. We craft each piece to complement not just your saree, but your personality.
