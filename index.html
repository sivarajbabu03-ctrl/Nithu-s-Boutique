Our Story

Born from a
love of craft.

Nithu's Boutique was born from a deep passion for India's timeless textile heritage. Nestled in Perumbakkam, Chennai, we curate sarees sourced directly from master weavers — from the regal Kanjivaram silk to the breezy Chanderi cotton.

Our blouse boutique is where tradition meets personal expression. Every blouse is crafted to complement not just your saree, but your personality — whether you prefer classic elegance or contemporary flair.
